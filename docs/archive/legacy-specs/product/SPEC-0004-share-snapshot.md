# SPEC-0004: Share Snapshot

## Overview
Allows users to export and share a specific decision outcome or blueprint with stakeholders or on social media.

## Share Decision Snapshot
A "Share" button generates a unique, view-only URL or a high-quality image card (snapshot) of the decision results.

## Share Card Content
- **Decision Title**: The core question being asked.
- **The Verdict**: The primary recommendation.
- **Risk Score**: The calculated risk level.
- **Confidence Level**: The agent alignment percentage.
- **SolveOS Branding**: Subtle link to "Create your own decision".

## Viral Loop Goal
By sharing professional-looking decision snapshots, users demonstrate their strategic thinking to others, while simultaneously driving organic traffic to SolveOS.

## Privacy Considerations
- **PII Scrubbing**: Option to hide sensitive company names or financial figures.
- **Public vs. Private**: Snapshots are private by default; users must explicitly "Publicize" to generate a shareable link.
- **Expiration**: Option to set snapshots to expire after a certain period.
