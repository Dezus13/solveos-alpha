# USER-STORIES-0001: Founder Decisions

## Scenarios

### 1. Founder deciding whether to start a company
- **Context**: "I have a stable job but a great idea for an AI-native CRM. Should I quit and raise a seed round?"
- **AI Role**: Analyze the opportunity cost, market timing, and personal risk profile.
- **Outcome**: A blueprint showing clear "Success Milestones" vs "Failure Triggers".

### 2. Founder deciding pricing
- **Context**: "We are at 1,000 beta users. Should we launch with a flat €20/month fee or a usage-based model?"
- **AI Role**: Simulate revenue growth under both models and predict churn based on current user behavior.
- **Outcome**: A recommendation for the model with the highest LTV (Lifetime Value).

### 3. Founder deciding market entry
- **Context**: "We have limited resources. Should we focus on the US market first or dominate our home EU market?"
- **AI Role**: Compare regulatory hurdles, competitive density, and CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) estimates.
- **Outcome**: A prioritized rollout roadmap.

## Acceptance Criteria
- User feels "challenged" by the Skeptic agent.
- User discovers at least one risk they hadn't previously considered.
- The blueprint provides a "The Verdict" that is clear and non-obvious.
